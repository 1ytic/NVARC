<rules_summary>
Expand each color region until it meets another color region, but do not overwrite the border.
</rules_summary>
<input_generation>
Input grids contain colored cells. The transformation operates on color patterns and relationships.
</input_generation>
<solution_steps>
1. Identify contiguous regions of the same color.
2. Expand each region by one layer, until it encounters a region of a different color or the grid border.
3. Do not modify the pixels on the border of the grid.
</solution_steps>
<key_insight>
Colors spread to fill adjacent areas of the same color.
</key_insight>
<puzzle_concepts>
- boundary
- color_mapping
- connected
- filling
- mirroring
</puzzle_concepts>
