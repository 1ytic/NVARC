<rules_summary>
Some non-zero numbers flanked by non-zero numbers, above or below the zero lines in the central column are replaced with 9.
</rules_summary>
<input_generation>
Input grids are provided as examples. The transformation rule must be inferred from the training examples.
</input_generation>
<solution_steps>
1. Identify the central column between the horizontal zero lines.
2. For each row in the central column, if the value is a non-zero number flanked by other non-zero numbers, change it to 9.
</solution_steps>
<key_insight>
The grids contain horizontal lines of zeros.
</key_insight>
