<rules_summary>
Colors expand outward from their initial locations until they encounter another color or the edge of the grid.
</rules_summary>
<input_generation>
Input grids contain colored cells. The transformation operates on color patterns and relationships.
</input_generation>
<solution_steps>
1. For each non-zero color, expand the color to adjacent empty cells horizontally and vertically.
2. Stop expanding when encountering another color or the edge of the grid.
</solution_steps>
<key_insight>
The grid contains multiple color values including 0.
</key_insight>

- filling
